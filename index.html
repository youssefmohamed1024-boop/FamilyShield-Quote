Coverage Options

Find the Policy That Fits Your Life

Every family is different. Explore the main types of life insurance to understand what makes sense for you.

Most Popular

Term Life Insurance

Coverage for a specific period — typically 10, 20, or 30 years. Term is often the most affordable option and ideal for income replacement during your working years.

  • Affordable premiums, especially when you're young
  • Large death benefit to replace income
  • Fixed premiums for the full policy term
  • Some policies are convertible to permanent coverage
Get Term Life Quotes
Starting from ~$18/mo for $500K · healthy 30-year-old
Lifelong Coverage

Whole Life Insurance

Permanent coverage that lasts your entire life, with a cash value component that grows over time. Often used for estate planning and wealth transfer.

  • Guaranteed death benefit regardless of when you pass
  • Builds cash value you can borrow against
  • Premiums stay level for life
  • Useful for estate planning and legacy goals
Get Whole Life Quotes
Starting from ~$120/mo for $250K whole life policy
Seniors & Families

Final Expense Insurance

Designed to cover funeral costs and end-of-life expenses. Smaller benefit amounts with simplified underwriting — often available to those who may not qualify elsewhere.

  • Coverage from $5,000 to $50,000
  • Simplified or guaranteed acceptance
  • No medical exam typically required
  • Permanently relieves family of funeral burdens
Get Final Expense Quotes
Starting from ~$30/mo for $10K final expense coverage
Fast Approval

No-Exam Life Insurance

Skip the medical exam and get covered faster. These policies use health questionnaires instead of physical exams — ideal for busy people who want quick, easy coverage.

  • No blood draw or physical exam needed
  • Approval often within days, not weeks
  • Available in term and permanent options
  • Great option for coverage up to $1 million+
