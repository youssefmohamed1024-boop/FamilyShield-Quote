What Is FamilyShield Quotes?

FamilyShield Quotes is an online lead generation and quote comparison platform for life insurance. We are not a licensed insurance company, insurance agency, or insurance broker. We do not underwrite, sell, or bind insurance policies.

How We Generate Revenue

FamilyShield Quotes may receive compensation when consumers submit quote requests through this website and their information is shared with licensed insurance agents, agencies, or marketing partners. This compensation may influence how and where information is presented on this site, but it does not influence the integrity of the information we provide or our editorial content.

Compensation we receive does not affect the cost of any insurance product to you. You will not pay more or less for insurance by using this website versus going directly to an insurer or agent.

Lead Generation Disclosure

When you submit a quote request form on this website, you are providing your contact information and insurance preferences to FamilyShield Quotes. This information may be shared with one or more of the following:

  • Licensed life insurance agents operating in your state
  • Licensed insurance agencies or brokers
  • Marketing partners or intermediaries who connect consumers with licensed insurance professionals

By submitting a form, you consent to being contacted by these parties via phone (including potentially automated calls), text message, and email, regarding life insurance products and services.

No Guarantee of Insurance

Submitting a quote request does not guarantee that you will be offered, approved for, or able to purchase any specific insurance product. Coverage availability, pricing, and terms vary by insurer, state, and individual eligibility factors including age, health, and policy type.
